# 🎓 BunkMate  
### Attendance. But smarter.

BunkMate is a student-first attendance calculator that tells you **when you can skip** — and when you really shouldn’t.

No guesswork.  
No last-minute panic.  
No blind bunking.

Just clean math, clear margins, and full control.

---

## 🚀 Why BunkMate?

Most attendance apps feel like school software.  
BunkMate feels like a **tool students actually want**.

It’s built for people who:
- think before they skip  
- like knowing the numbers  
- want control, not surprises  

BunkMate doesn’t encourage bunking —  
it encourages **bunking smart**.

---

## 🧠 What it does

- 📊 Calculates your **current attendance**
- 🧮 Tells you **how many classes you can skip**
- 🚨 Warns you when you’re entering the danger zone
- 📅 Supports **weekly schedules**
- 🌗 Light / Dark mode (yes, students care)
- 💾 Resume last data or auto-paste from clipboard
- 🔔 Optional alerts & reminders

All logic runs **locally**.  
No unnecessary data collection. No nonsense.

---

## 🔐 Privacy by design

BunkMate:
- stores data **on-device**
- does not track you
- does not sell anything about you

Your attendance is your business.

---

## 🧩 Tech Stack

Built with tools that are fast, modern, and boring (in a good way):

- **Flutter** (Material 3)
- **Provider** for state management
- **Hive** for local storage
- **Firebase** (Remote Config, optional services)
- **WorkManager** for background checks
- **Google Mobile Ads** (carefully placed, non-annoying)

Performance and UX were treated as first-class features.
